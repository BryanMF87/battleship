Game Instructions

Arrange your ships in friendly waters. Once game starts fire at enemy ships by clicking on squares in enemy waters. The enemy will fire back at your ships inbetween your shots. First player to sink all enemy ships wins.

Air carrier: 5 hits

Battlecruiser: 4 hits

Destoyer: 3 hits

Submarine: 2 hits

Patrol boat: 1 hit
